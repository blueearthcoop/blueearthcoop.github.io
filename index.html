Our Mission

We take pride in our craft and want to provide the very best for our friends and neighbors. Everything we bake starts with our natural sourdough starter, traditional ingredients, and a whole lot of love.

Every dollar supports the restoration and care of the historic Blue Earth Co-Operative Creamery building on Main Street, a landmark built in 1900.
