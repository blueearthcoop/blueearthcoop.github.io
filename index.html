Blue Earth Cooperative

Homemade sourdough and baked goods from a historic creamery in Blue Earth, Minnesota.

We're a cottage bakery baking everything from scratch with our natural sourdough starter, traditional ingredients, and a whole lot of love. Every purchase supports the restoration of our 125-year-old creamery building on Main Street.
