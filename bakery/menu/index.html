Our Menu

Everything we bake starts with our natural sourdough starter. Check our online ordering page for what's available this week, or follow us on Facebook for pop-up locations.

Sourdough Focaccia

Our signature line. Savory and sweet varieties, all built on our tangy sourdough base.

  • Jalapeño Cheddar — Sharp cheddar, fresh jalapeño slices
  • Green Onion & Curd — Green onions and cheese curds folded into the dough
  • Smoked Gruyere & Candied Red Onion — Savory, slightly sweet, smoky
  • Cinnamon Roll — Classic cinnamon roll sweetness meets sourdough tang
  • Lemon Blueberry — Tangy lemon and sweet blueberries
  • Hawaiian Pineapple — Homemade pineapple filling, passion fruit, orange, and guava glaze

Sourdough Breads & Rolls

  • Soft Sourdough Sandwich Bread — Tender crumb, mild tang
  • Sourdough Dinner Rolls — Soft, fluffy, full of flavor
  • Sourdough Hawaiian Rolls — A hint of pineapple sweetness

Sweet Treats

  • Buttered Corn Cookie — Soft, buttery, intense corn flavor
  • Brown Sugar Sourdough Cookie — Nutty, complex, addicting
  • Texas Sheet Cake — Chocolate, cinnamon, Mexican vanilla
  • Peanut Butter & Chocolate Sheet Cake — Rich peanut butter, fudge frosting
  • Apple Cider Sheet Cake — Fluffy cake with tender apple bits
  • Roasted Strawberry Sheet Cake — Tangy and sweet

Menu varies by bake day. Not everything is available every week.
